✅ 1. Cities with no streets

You want cities that don’t have any rows in street referencing them.

SELECT c.id, c.name
FROM city c
WHERE NOT EXISTS (
    SELECT 1
    FROM street s
    WHERE s.city_id = c.id
)
ORDER BY c.name ASC;

Why this works:

NOT EXISTS is the safest way to check absence (better than LEFT JOIN + NULL in most cases)
It avoids issues with duplicates or joins
