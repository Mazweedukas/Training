✅ 3. Products priced above their category average

We compare each product’s price against the average price of products in the same category.

SELECT p.id, pt.title, p.price
FROM product p
JOIN product_title pt ON pt.id = p.product_title_id
WHERE p.price > (
    SELECT AVG(p2.price)
    FROM product p2
    JOIN product_title pt2 ON pt2.id = p2.product_title_id
    WHERE pt2.product_category_id = pt.product_category_id
)
ORDER BY p.id ASC;
